Gray Wolf

A single wolf can consume 20 pounds of meat at a sitting.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus
  • Average Length: 3.4-5.2 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 6-13 years
  • Habitat: Diverse, ranges from deserts to tundras

Also known as the Timber Wolf and Eastern Wolf, gray wolves are found in a variety of habitats from grasslands to artic tundras. They reach about 3.4-5.2 feet in lenght and have a thick gray coat that is often flecked with black and yellow.
